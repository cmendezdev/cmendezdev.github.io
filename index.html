About

I'm a backend engineer based in Houston, Texas, with seven years of experience building services that are fast, reliable, and easy to reason about. I earned my B.S. in Computer Science from the University of Houston in 2018 and have since worked across infrastructure, cloud, and financial systems at BMC Software, DigitalOcean, HashiCorp, and now JPMorgan Chase.

My philosophy is simple: good software is boring. It does what it says, fails gracefully, and doesn't surprise you at 3 AM. I gravitate toward Go for its clarity, but I'll reach for the right tool for the job.

When I'm not writing code, you'll find me analyzing chess openings or hunting down the perfect espresso in Houston's coffee scene.
